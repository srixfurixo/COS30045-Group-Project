Mortality against Number of Vaccination Doses

Introduction

During the COVID-19 pandemic, vaccinations became a central tool in reducing mortality and controlling the virus's spread. Five vaccines gained widespread global use: Pfizer-BioNTech, Sputnik V, Moderna, AstraZeneca, and Johnson & Johnson. This analysis compares the mortality rates in relation to the number of doses administered of these five vaccines, aiming to understand their impact on reducing fatalities across countries.

Analysis

Comparing COVID-19 mortality rates alongside vaccination data for the five vaccines shows a general trend of slightly reducing mortality.
