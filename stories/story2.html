Healthcare Expenditure by Health Function (Radial View)

Introduction

The COVID-19 pandemic was a formidable test for healthcare systems worldwide, exposing vulnerabilities and gaps in preparedness. This visualization illustrates healthcare expenditure related to various health functions for each country, highlighting the areas with the highest spending.

Analysis

This chart shows the distribution of COVID-19-related spending categories per country. The inner segments represent lower values, while the outer segments show larger expenditures. The radial layout allows for a clear comparison of spending across countries.

Conclusion

In many cases, countries with high expenditures in certain health functions could demonstrate more robust responses to the pandemic. However, multiple factors, including policy and healthcare infrastructure, influence outcomes.
