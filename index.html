Welcome to Formula Data

We Visualise Data Fast!

Our project aims to provide insightful visualizations on various aspects of the COVID-19 pandemic. Through these visualizations, we seek to highlight the impact of the pandemic on different sectors and populations, and to explore the relationships between various factors and COVID-19 outcomes.

We aim to achieve the following:

  • Highlight the severity of COVID-19 globally
  • Explore the relationship between healthcare expenditure and mortality rates
  • Analyze the impact of different vaccination brands on mortality
  • Examine the availability of ICU beds or Hospital Admission and its effect on mortality
  • Visualize vaccination coverage against population to see if higher vaccination reduces Covid-19 impact.

Below, you will find a series of visualizations that delve into different topics related to COVID-19. Click on each visualization to explore the data in more detail.

Timeline Map of COVID-19 Mortality Worldwide

This visualisation aims to showcase the severity of COVID-19 on the populace globally throughtout 2020 to 2022. As such, it highlights the mortality worldwide to paint a sober image to remind us all of the danger and importance of having good disease control globally.

Healthcare Expenditure against Mortality Rate

This visualization explores the relationship between a country's healthcare expenditure as a percentage of GDP and its COVID-19 mortality rate from 2020 to 2024. It seeks to determine whether increased healthcare spending correlates with lower COVID-19 mortality rates, suggesting a potential impact of financial resources, or if other factors influence the outcomes.

Mortality against Types of Vaccination Brands

This visualization analyzes the relationship between COVID-19 mortality rates and the brand of vaccine administered to individuals prior to their death. It aims to investigate whether certain vaccine brands are associated with differing mortality outcomes.

Total ICU Beds Available Compared To Mortality

This visualization examines the relationship between ICU bed availability and COVID-19 mortality rates. It seeks to determine if regions with greater ICU capacity experience fewer deaths, suggesting the impact of critical care resources on patient outcomes.

Population vs Cummulative Vaccinations

This visualization examines the relationship between a country's population size and the cumulative number of COVID-19 vaccines administered. It seeks to visualize vaccination coverage and progress across different populations, potentially revealing trends or disparities in vaccine distribution and uptake.
